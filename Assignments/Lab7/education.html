My Education

Stevens Institute of Technology

Favorite Class - CS615: Systems Administration

Memory - On the day of my CAL 103 final exam, the fire alarm went off in my dorm and I ended up being late to the exam. While this is not my favorite memory, the stress was very memorable!

The Beacon School

Favorite Class - Physics

Memory - Instead of a homeroom, my school had a once a week advisory group meeting with our advisor, who we would stick with for all four years. One day, our advisor was talking about how some kids had been caught vaping in the bathroom. The memorable part stems from her response, where she said if you wanted to be cool you should 'just smoke a real cigarette like real man. I found this irrationally funny.

Manhattan East School for Arts & Academic

Favorite Class - U.S. History

Memory - In 8th grade, some of my friends and I started playing Dungeons & Dragons with our 7th grade history teacher during our recess after lunch. It was very fun to learn how to play the game, and it was also fun to improvise depening on how we rolled the dice.
